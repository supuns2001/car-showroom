What We Offer

Comprehensive automotive services designed for Sri Lankan buyers

🏦

Vehicle Leasing Assistance

We partner with all major commercial banks and licensed finance companies in Sri Lanka — including People's Bank, Commercial Bank, Nations Trust Bank, and LOLC — to arrange leasing facilities at competitive interest rates. Our finance specialists will compare packages and find the best deal for your income and credit profile, with repayment periods of up to 7 years and financing of up to 85% of the vehicle value.

🛡️

Insurance Arrangements

Don't waste time shopping for insurance. Our dedicated team will obtain quotations from multiple leading insurers including Sri Lanka Insurance, Ceylinco, and Allianz, ensuring you receive the broadest comprehensive or third-party fire & theft cover at the lowest premium available. We handle all documentation and ensure your policy is active before you drive away.

📋

Vehicle Registration Services

Navigating DMT (Department of Motor Traffic) can be time-consuming. Our experienced team manages the entire registration process on your behalf — from preparing all forms and receipts to attending the DMT office for transfers and new registrations. We ensure your vehicle is legally registered in your name with minimum hassle and delay.

🔄

Trade-In Options

Ready to upgrade your current vehicle? Bring it to LankaDrive for a free, no-obligation valuation by our expert assessors. We assess condition, mileage, market demand, and current prices to offer you a fair trade-in value. You can then apply this amount directly toward your next purchase, reducing the finance required and getting you into your dream car sooner.

🚗

Test Drive Booking

We believe the best way to choose a vehicle is to drive it. Book a test drive with any vehicle in our showroom at your convenience — including early mornings and weekends. Our team will guide you on the key features and performance characteristics to look for, ensuring you feel fully confident in your decision before signing anything.

🔍

Vehicle Inspection Reports

Every vehicle we sell is backed by a comprehensive inspection report covering the engine, transmission, suspension, bodywork, electronics, and safety systems. For reconditioned imports from Japan, we provide the original Auction Sheet indicating the vehicle's condition grade at the time of export. Customers may also request an independent third-party inspection at their cost.
