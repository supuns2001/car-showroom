Our Story

Built on Integrity,
Driven by Excellence

LankaDrive Auto Sales (Pvt) Ltd was founded in 2012 with a simple mission: to make buying a quality vehicle in Sri Lanka transparent, trustworthy, and accessible to every family and professional.

Located in the heart of Colombo, we specialise in brand new imported vehicles, Japanese reconditioned cars, and pre-owned registered vehicles — all thoroughly inspected and fairly valued. Over 12 years, we have sold more than 500 vehicles to satisfied customers across the island.

Whether you are a first-time buyer looking for an economical city car or a business owner seeking a premium SUV, our knowledgeable team is here to guide you every step of the way.

Mission & Vision

🎯 Our Mission

To provide Sri Lankan customers with access to high-quality, fairly priced vehicles backed by honest advice, reliable after-sales support, and a seamless buying experience that they will recommend to friends and family.

🌟 Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's most trusted and respected automotive dealership — a company synonymous with integrity, customer care, and a world-class vehicle buying experience from Colombo to every corner of the island.
